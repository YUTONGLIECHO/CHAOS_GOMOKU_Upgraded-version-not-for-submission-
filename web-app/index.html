HOW TO PLAY

GOAL: connect five of your black discs in a row — flat, upright, or diagonal — before the AI does.

CAMERA (MOUSE): left-drag to orbit, wheel to zoom, right/middle-drag to pan.

CAMERA (TRACKPAD): one-finger drag to orbit, two-finger drag to pan, pinch (or two-finger scroll) to zoom. Tap to place.

CAMERA (TOUCH): one finger orbits, pinch zooms, two fingers pan.

PLACING: tap an empty intersection. A quick tap places; a drag just moves the camera, so you never misfire after spinning the board.

TURN: place one piece, then optionally fire one skill. Most skills end your turn and go on cooldown.

SKILLS:

  • ☄ YEET METEOR — launch one enemy piece into orbit. (cd 5)
  • ⌖ FINDERS KEEPERS — teleport an enemy piece to a random empty cell. (cd 4)
  • ⚑ SPRING CLEANING — sweep 1–3 random enemy pieces off the board. (cd 7)
  • ❄ ABSOLUTE ZERO — freeze the foe's skills for one turn. (cd 5)
  • ⎌ CTRL + Z — undo the opponent's last full turn. (once)
  • ⇄ CORPORATE RESTRUCTURING — swap the colour of every piece. (once)
  • ┻ TABLE FLIP — clear the whole board through furniture violence. (once)
